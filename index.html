Biography

I am an early career postdoctoral research fellow in Statistical Bioinformatics. I am interested in exploring emerging technologies such as scATAC-Seq and long-read scRNA-Seq to answer important biological questions. I have been in the field of bioinformatics for more than 6 years. I thoroughly enjoy challenges I face as a bioinformatician and learning to solve problems in analysing high-throughput data in a collaborative environment. Other than doing research, I love participating in extra-curricular activities. Currently I am in the executive baord of the Postdoctoral Association of WEHI. While I am not at work, I am a daughter of two extraordinary parents, a loving (hopefully!) wife and a proud mother of a wonderful boy.
